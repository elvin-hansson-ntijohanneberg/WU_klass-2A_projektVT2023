Amanita Design

Amanita Design is a Czech independent video game development studio known for creating visually stunning and whimsical point-and-click adventure games. The studio was founded in 2003 by Jakub Dvorsky and has since gained international recognition for its distinctive art style and narrative-driven gameplay.

Some of Amanita Design's most popular games include:

Machinarium (2009) - a puzzle-solving adventure game set in a dystopian robot city

Botanicula (2012) - a game about a group of plant creatures on a mission to save their home from dark spider-like invaders

Samorost 3 (2016) - a surreal exploration game set in a universe filled with strange creatures and mystical landscapes

Amanita Design's games are known for their hand-drawn graphics, charming animations, and playful sound design. The studio often incorporates environmental storytelling, non-linear gameplay, and creative puzzles that challenge the player's problem-solving skills.

Overall, Amanita Design has become a beloved studio in the indie gaming community, earning critical acclaim and numerous awards for their innovative and engaging games.
